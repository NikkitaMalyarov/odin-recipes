Borscht Recipe

Description

Borscht is a traditional Eastern European soup made primarily with beets, giving it a rich red color. It typically includes vegetables like cabbage, potatoes, and carrots, with some versions containing meat. The soup has a tangy flavor and is often served with sour cream and dill. It can be enjoyed hot or cold.

Ingredients

  • Beets
  • Cabbage
  • Potatoes
  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Vegetable or beef broth
  • Bay leaves
  • Dill
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Vinegar or Lemon juice
  • Sugar
  • Oil or butter
  • Sour cream

Steps

  1. Saut√© onions, carrots, and garlic in oil.
  2. Add beets and tomato paste, then cook for a few minutes.
  3. Pour in broth and add potatoes and bay leaves.
  4. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  5. Add cabbage, salt, pepper, vinegar, and sugar.
  6. Cook until the cabbage is soft.
  7. Serve with sour cream and fresh dill.
